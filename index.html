Dr. Alberto Testoni

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in Responsible NLP and ML for Healthcare (CaRe-NLP project) at Amsterdam UMC, part of the NLP4Health Lab led by prof. Iacer Calixto. My research focuses on making generative AI models more reliable, specifically on uncertainty estimation, calibration, and safe deployment of Large Language Models (LLMs) in healthcare. Before joining Amsterdam UMC, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Institute for Logic, Language and Computation - University of Amsterdam, working with Raquel Fernández on evaluating and improving the multimodal and interactive capabilities of LLMs. I hold a PhD in Computer Science from the University of Trento (supervised by Raffaella Bernardi), an MSc in Cognitive Science from CIMeC - University of Trento, and a BSc in Computer Science from the University of Bologna.
