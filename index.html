Alberto Testoni

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in Responsible NLP and ML for Healthcare (CaRe-NLP) at Amsterdam UMC and part of the NLP4Health Lab led by prof. Iacer Calixto. Previously, I worked as a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Institute for Logic, Language and Computation - University of Amsterdam and part of the Dialogue Modeling group (led by Raquel Fernández). I got my PhD in Computer Science from the IECS International Doctoral School at the University of Trento, working under the supervision of Raffaella Bernardi. Before that, I obtained an M.Sc. in Cognitive Science from CIMeC - University of Trento and a B.Sc. in Computer Science from the University of Bologna. My work lies at the intersection of various fields, including Natural Language Processing, Artificial Intelligence, and Cognitive Science. Specifically, my research focuses on developing more effective NLP systems inspired by human language use, especially in multimodal visually-grounded settings.
