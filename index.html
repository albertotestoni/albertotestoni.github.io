Alberto Testoni

I am a Ph.D. student at the ICT International Doctoral School - UNITN, working under the supervision of Raffaella Bernardi. I obtained a M.Sc. in Cognitive Science from CIMeC - Center for Mind/Brain Sciences - UNITN and I hold a B.Sc. in Computer Science from Alma Mater Studiorum - University of Bologna. My work lies at the intersection between different fields, such as Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision and Cognitive Science; in particular, my research focues on training Deep Neural Networks from multi-modal data (language, vision and sound).
