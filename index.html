Alberto Testoni

I am a 3rd year Ph.D. student at the ICT International Doctoral School (University of Trento), working under the supervision of Raffaella Bernardi. I am currently an research scientist intern at Amazon Alexa AI in Berlin (Natural Language Understanding). I obtained an M.Sc. in Cognitive Science from CIMeC-UniTN (Center for Mind/Brain Sciences) and I hold a B.Sc. in Computer Science from the University of Bologna. My work lies at the intersection between different fields, such as Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision and Cognitive Science; in particular, my research focuses on training Deep Neural Networks from multi-modal data (language, vision and sound). Check out my full list of publications and feel free to get in touch!
